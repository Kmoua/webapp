Bathroom Style Ideas

Bathroom Style Ideas

Looking for some bathroom decor inspiration? Here are some beautiful bathrooms to get your decoration gears going. Maybe you'll glean an idea or two for your own home?

  • White modern bathroom: Clean lines, white, and a few organic touches are all that's needed.
  • Black powder room: White isn't the only thing that you can use in a powder room. Dare to go dark by painting it black---but don't forget to add color with some beautiful art! Black is always classic and elegant.
  • Walk-in shower with pebble tiles: Remember that last time washing off the salt from your skin by adding beach-inspired elements to your bathroom. Pebbles are a great way to start!
  • Fresh traditional bathroom: Lots of white with a touch of green, tons of light with a bamboo roman shade: these all work together to make a nice, refreshing bathroom in which to wash the day off.

Storage Ideas

Storage Ideas

Here's what you can do to maximize storage and save space.

  1. Try installing a dish-drying cabinet directly above the sink.
  2. Make use of space in front of your sink with these false front drawers.
  3. Store your foil and plastic wrap in a mounted magazine rack.
  4. Use pull-out drawers for all your bread and root veggies.
  5. Install pull-out hampers in a bathroom or laundry room.
  6. Use a picture ledge next to or behind your bed for phones and glasses.
